## Key facts

- **How to apply:** Single $70 application covers PhD + MS auto-consideration; indicate interest in MS in personal statement; does not reduce PhD admission chances
- **Degree options:** Thesis (Plan A, 18–24 months) or Non-thesis (30 hours + final essay; can complete in as few as 9 months); thesis is strongly preferred for PhD pathway
- **Funding reality:** Competitive internal fellowships and TA/RA positions; not guaranteed; Frank should email target PIs to express interest in funded TA/RA positions at time of MS application
- **Deadline:** Same as PhD (~Dec 1); single application
- **Priority action:** Frank's PhD application already covers this; no extra effort needed beyond indicating MS interest in the SoP

---

## PhD pathway from Rochester MS

1. Thesis with Otte or Vamivakas + strong LoR → reapply to JILA, Maryland, Cornell, or MIT with Rochester thesis publication
2. Internal conversion to Rochester PhD (verify current policy with graduate coordinator)
3. Rochester MS + networking with optics community through faculty connections positions Frank extremely well for a second PhD cycle

---

## Red flags

- Funding not guaranteed — if unfunded, tuition cost must be verified; without funding, financial viability depends on external sources (CSC scholarship, family support)
- No-advisor-match policy for Rochester (students join groups after Year 1 coursework) applies to MS students too — thesis requires finding an advisor after arrival
