## Key facts

- **Correct program:** Apply to **MS in Quantum Science & Technology** (NYU Tandon; newly launched) rather than MS in Physics; this program is designed for quantum career preparation and is more aligned with Frank's goals
- **Duration:** ~2 years; 30 credits; thesis/project option (6 or 9 credits)
- **Telecom quantum networking:** NYU Tandon demonstrated 10-mile quantum entanglement distribution over standard NYC telecom fiber (2023) — this is a direct quantum networking milestone that Frank can reference in his SoP; the research environment is actively engaged in quantum networking at the hardware level
- **NYC quantum ecosystem:** Manhattan and Brooklyn quantum startup cluster (Qunnect was founded by NYU researchers); proximity to quantum companies for part-time research or co-op
- **Deadline:** ~December 30 (NYU GSAS Physics; verify for Quantum S&T MS specifically)
- **Funding priority:** NYU Tandon funding for MS students is not guaranteed; Frank must secure RA or external fellowship (CSC) to make this financially viable

## PhD pathway from NYU Tandon MS

1. NYC quantum networking research + thesis → re-apply to Columbia APAM PhD, Cornell, or Maryland with proven quantum networking research
2. Qunnect and other NYC-based quantum networking companies for co-op → industry quantum networking role (alternative to PhD if desired)
3. NYU faculty connections to broader NYC quantum community (Columbia, Princeton, NIST)
