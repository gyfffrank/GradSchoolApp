## Funding details
- **Years of guaranteed funding:** 2 years TA guaranteed; 2–3 years external (RA) expected; total 4–5 years typical
- **Annual stipend:** $37,783/year ($28,337 AY + $9,446 summer) — confirmed for AY 2026–27; full tuition covered; stipend set annually by RPI Board of Trustees based on Troy/Rensselaer County COL
- **Funding source:** TA first 2 years; RA with advisor years 3–5
- **International-student eligibility:** Eligible for standard TA/RA; NSF GRFP ineligible (non-citizen)
- **COL note:** Troy NY (Rensselaer County) COL is very low — one of the lowest in the Northeast; $37,783 in Troy has significantly more purchasing power than $35k in Boston or $43k in NYC; Frank would live comfortably on this stipend
