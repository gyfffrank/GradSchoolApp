## Target PIs

### Quantum Optics & Photonics Research Cluster
Stevens has an identified quantum optics & photonics research cluster including:
- **LiNbO₃ nanophotonics and quantum frequency conversion** — directly relevant to quantum networking (LiNbO₃ is the key platform for photon-pair generation and frequency conversion in quantum optical communications)
- **Quantum dots** — single-photon sources relevant to quantum networking hardware
- **Fast modulated lasers** — relevant to quantum communication timing and coherent control
- The Center for Quantum Science and Engineering operates a **hybrid quantum-communications network** on the Stevens campus — a live quantum networking testbed is an unusual resource at this tier

Specific faculty names for this cluster were not confirmed in available search data. **Frank must contact the Stevens graduate admissions office or Center for Quantum Science and Engineering directly to identify which faculty are in this cluster and whether they are actively recruiting PhD students in quantum networking / quantum photonics before submitting an application.**

### Chunlei Qu — Assistant Professor of Physics
- **Group:** Qu Lab (AMO theory, topological condensed matter, quantum optics)
- **Group size:** ~2–3 students
- **Research:** Theoretical AMO physics — BEC dynamics in synthetic gauge fields, exceptional points in non-Hermitian systems, optical Hopfield networks
  - *Optical Hopfield neural networks with enhanced storage capacity* — Optics Letters, 2026
  - *Robust pattern retrieval in an optical Hopfield neural network* — Optics Letters, 2025
  - *Collective oscillations of BECs in a synthetic magnetic field* — Physical Review Research, 2025
- **Frank's specific hook:** Qu's exceptional point / non-Hermitian physics work (related to Harris at Yale) is intellectually adjacent to Frank's nonlinear dynamics background. His optical Hopfield network work connects structured optical fields to information processing. However, Qu is a theorist — Frank would need co-advising with an experimental PI from the quantum photonics cluster.
